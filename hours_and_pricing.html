All dogs must have the following vaccinations before they will be allowed to board:

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Bordetella

Hours of Operation

For Picking up and Dropping off your pet for boarding

  • Monday through Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. and 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
  • Saturday: 9:00 a.m. - 10:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
  • Sunday: 11:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. and 4:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
Holiday Hours are 11:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. for the following holidays:
  • Labor Day
  • Memorial Day
  • 4th of July
  • Christmas Eve
  • New Years Eve
There will be no picking up or dropping off on the following holidays:
  • Easter Sunday
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day
  • New Years Day

Boarding Pricing

DOG BOARDING

  • First dog: $22.00 per day
  • Second dog: $17.00 per day
  • Three or more dogs: $17.00 per dog per day

CAT BOARDING

  • One cat: $11.00 per day
  • Two cats: $20.00 per day
  • Three or more cats: $9.00 per cat per day

Dog Grooming - by appointment only

Pricing can very based on breed and size. Please call for pricing and to hear about our various discounts offered such as our active duty, veteran and first responder discounts.
